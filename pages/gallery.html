Sometimes I like to travel and take Pictures.

Here are a few pictures that i've taken with some context. enjoy!!

Danube River

This picture was taken in budapest, Hungary. budapest is one of the most beautiful citites in europe and the Danube River falls right in the middle of the city, the atmosphere is always enjoyble there no matter the time, there are also many other attractions in this city but i felt that having a picture of the second largest river in europe was worth sharing.

Como River Ducks

This picture was taken in northern Italy, the place is called lake como and it was one of the most beautiful places i ever visited, the weather there is always nice and the area is very calming and sweet, even those ducks were just chilling there with the warm lake view!

Yoyogi park

This picture was taken in Tokyo, Japan, the place is called Yoyogi park and it is located in the heart of tokyo. it almsot feels like a forest in the middle of a huge city and usually weddings are held at this place because of it's natural beauty.

Aeigna Island

This picture was taken in Aeigna island in Greece, it is one of many islands that Greece have, the island is filled with greek history and it is 2 hours away from the port of athens. fun fact about this island is that cars are not allowed on it and you can only use donkeys to move around.
